AVENTURATE

Aventurate is an agency specialized in trips and excursions with adventure activities. It is aimed at the entire public, from the smallest, to the oldest, since we have activities of all kinds, from extreme sports, to more gentle excursions so that everyone can enjoy it.

EXPERIENCES FOR ADULTS

In aventurate we have a great offer of extreme sports, such as diving, with which you can enjoy with your friends or family.

We have a great variety of experiences, all of them one day, in which you will enjoy as a child.

TRIPS FOR ADULTS

In aventurate we have adult trips, such as skiing, giving you the option to choose the time you want to spend outside the home.

You can choose between a day trip, or a weekend, but they will always be excursions to enjoy as children.

EXPERIENCES IN FAMILY

In aventurate we have a wide range of experiences to enjoy with the whole family, both the youngest and the oldest.

You can choose between a day trip, or a weekend, but they will always be excursions to enjoy both the smallest and the oldest.
